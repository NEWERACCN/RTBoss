Value Engines · VE-MIP

MIP — Managed Intelligent Provider Services

New Era IT's current revenue foundation. MIP turns every IT event, service request, and infrastructure alert into a structured Fact — context added, asset linked, resolution routed through the Agentic Factory. Four engines govern how MIP wins clients, delivers services, improves its product, and manages physical infrastructure.

◈
Four engines, one pillar. Growth wins clients. Fulfillment delivers the promise. Innovation improves the product. Logistics keeps operations running. Every pillar runs all four — the engine type never changes, the application context does.
◈
Growth Engine
How MIP acquires and expands clients

MIP Growth converts market presence into recurring MRR. The engine runs three tiers — Trial ($2–4k), Standard ($4–8k), Strategic ($8–15k) — with each tier designed to demonstrate value fast and create natural expansion paths upward. Growth is not just new logos; it is expanding wallet share within existing accounts as each new Mini-OS domain is activated.

Once a client's operations run through LiveOps, switching cost is near-zero to add any new Mini-OS domain.
