Finance OS

AP/AR, forecasting, tax compliance, and profit management. Finance OS is the reference implementation for the entire Mini-OS pattern — every other domain follows this same structure. Built on Profit First methodology, governed by four Systems, executed by Genies, and run by recurring Streams.

? REFERENCE IMPLEMENTATION — Full Build Guide
?
Why Finance OS is first: Every business runs on money. Finance OS is also the most mature implementation — all Systems, Genies, and Streams are fully defined. It is the template every other Mini-OS is built from. Read this one fully before building any other domain.

System Definitions — What Each Governs
Profit First System

Governs how money flows through the business. Defines account structures (Income, OpEx, Tax, Owner Pay, Profit), allocation percentages, and the rules for every financial decision. Every Genie and Stream in Finance OS operates within these rules.

Modeling System

Defines the financial model structure — chart of accounts, cost centre taxonomy, revenue recognition rules, and intercompany treatment. This is the schema every report, forecast, and analysis is built on.

Forecasting System

Defines how forecasts are built — which inputs matter, how historical patterns are weighted, confidence interval methodology, and rolling horizon rules (7-day, 30-day, 12-month). The Budget Builder Genie and RTBI Planning component both operate within this framework.

Reporting System

Standards for what gets reported, to whom, in what format, and at what cadence. Defines P&L structure, board pack format, management dashboard layout, and AU tax reporting requirements.

Genie Detail — What Each Agent Does
Budget Builder

Invoked at the start of each planning cycle. Reads prior period actuals from Business Central, applies Profit First allocation rules, applies Forecasting System methodology, and outputs a draft budget for human review.

Hybrid WorkerOn-demand
End of Month

Runs the month-end close checklist: reconcile bank accounts, clear outstanding approvals, validate GL entries, produce management P&L per the Reporting System standard. Flags discrepancies for human review.

Hybrid WorkerStream-invoked
Tax Reporting

Calculates AU tax obligations (GST, income tax, payroll tax) per the Profit First System and Reporting System standards. Prepares lodgement-ready output. Never auto-lodges without human sign-off.

System Definitions — What Each Governs
Strategic Planning System

Governs how strategic direction is set, reviewed, and updated. Defines the planning cadence (annual strategy, quarterly OKR cycles, monthly reviews), the methodology for setting objectives, and the criteria for evaluating strategic options. Every initiative must trace back to a goal governed by this System.

Competitive Intelligence System

Defines how market signals are captured, categorised, and acted on. Rules for what constitutes a competitive event worth tracking, how intelligence is validated, how it maps to existing strategic positioning, and when it triggers a strategy review.

OKR Management System

The framework for how objectives are set, cascaded, measured, and evaluated. Defines OKR structure (Objective + 3-5 Key Results), scoring methodology (0.0–1.0), review cadence, and the rules for when an OKR should be updated, closed, or carried forward.

Invoked at each quarterly OKR checkpoint. Reads current KR scores from Dataverse, surfaces progress against targets, flags at-risk KRs, and prepares a structured review document for the leadership team. A Hybrid Worker process — Genie prepares, humans review and decide.

Hybrid WorkerEvent-triggered
Strategy Narrative Genie

Invoked when preparing board updates, investor communications, or all-hands messaging. Takes current strategic state from Dataverse, synthesises into a coherent narrative aligned with the Strategic Planning System's communication standards.

System Definitions — What Each Governs
Onboarding System

Defines the complete onboarding sequence — which steps must occur in what order, which are role-specific vs universal, what constitutes completion, and what tools and access are provisioned at each stage. Every new hire follows this System regardless of role or department.

Performance Management System

Governs how performance is observed, recorded, and acted on. Defines review cadence (probation, 6-month, annual), rating methodology, escalation criteria, performance improvement plan triggers, and the documentation standards for every performance event.

Compliance Tracking System

Ensures all employment obligations are met — Fair Work Act requirements, right-to-work verification, mandatory training completion, privacy policy acknowledgements, and certification renewals. Generates alerts before deadlines; logs all completions as COMPLIANCE_RECORD Digital Assets.

System Definitions — What Each Governs
Contract Management System

Governs the full contract lifecycle — intake, review, negotiation, execution, storage, and renewal. Defines contract types, review requirements by value and risk level, approval authority thresholds, and the standards for clause flagging and risk scoring.

Risk Register System

Defines how legal and contractual risks are identified, scored, tracked, and reported. Every flagged clause becomes an entry in the risk register. Governs escalation thresholds — which risks need legal counsel vs internal approval vs notification only.

Obligation Tracking System

Ensures every contractual obligation — payment terms, delivery milestones, reporting requirements, renewal options — is tracked against a timeline. Governs how DEADLINE and OBLIGATION Digital Assets are created, monitored, and closed when fulfilled.

Genie Detail — What Each Agent Does
Contract Review Genie

Invoked on contract_received Fact. Parses the document, identifies standard and non-standard clauses, scores risk per the Risk Register System criteria, and produces a structured review summary with flagged items highlighted for human lawyer or approver.

Hybrid WorkerEvent-triggered
Clause Risk Genie

Invoked when a clause_flagged Fact occurs. Deep-analyzes the specific clause, compares against standard positions and prior accepted variations, assesses enforceability under relevant jurisdiction, and recommends accept / negotiate / reject with reasoning.

Hybrid WorkerEvent-triggered
Deadline Alert Genie

Runs continuously against DEADLINE Digital Assets. Surfaces upcoming expirations, renewal windows, and obligation due dates at configured lead times (90/60/30/7 days). Routes alerts to the responsible owner with context from the parent CONTRACT.

System Definitions — What Each Governs
Pipeline Management System

Governs how opportunities move through the sales funnel — stage definitions, entry and exit criteria for each stage, required activities before progression, and stall detection rules (how many days without activity before an alert triggers). Every OPPORTUNITY Digital Asset follows this System.

Forecasting System

Defines how revenue is forecast — probability weightings by stage, historical close rate inputs, seasonal adjustment methodology, and the rolling forecast horizon. The Revenue Forecast Stream and RTBI Planning both operate within this framework.

Account Intelligence System

Governs how ACCOUNT Digital Assets are built and maintained — what intelligence is tracked (org chart, budget cycle, tech stack, relationship health), how it is updated, and how it informs deal strategy and renewal risk scoring.

Genie Detail — What Each Agent Does
Proposal Genie

Invoked on proposal_sent preparation. Reads the OPPORTUNITY Digital Asset, pulls account history from the ACCOUNT record, applies Pipeline Management System pricing rules, and generates a first-draft proposal document. Human tailors and approves before sending.

Hybrid WorkerOn-demand
Follow-Up Genie

Triggered by stall detection in the Pipeline Management System (no stage activity within threshold). Drafts a contextually appropriate follow-up message based on deal stage, last interaction, and account intelligence. Human reviews before send.

Hybrid WorkerEvent-triggered
Deal Coach Genie

Invoked on deal_lost or when a deal is flagged as at-risk. Analyzes the deal history from the Digital Twin, compares against won deals of similar profile, identifies where the deal deviated, and produces coaching recommendations for the sales owner.
