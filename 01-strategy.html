Tab 01 · RTBoss Core

The Real-Time Business Operating System

It is not software, not a methodology, and not just automation. It is a real-time, living future-of-work operating model that delivers peak performance, increased value, and measurable outcomes.

RTBoss Core Workflow
Facts->Dimensions->Digital Assets->Agentic Factory->Digital Workforce->Digital Twin->New Fact
Core Elements

Facts

What it is: A real event with a time, source, and owner.

Why it matters: Work should start from what actually happened.

Output: A recorded event that can be acted on.

Dimensions

What it is: Context fields on the fact: who, where, urgency, risk, value, and jurisdiction.

Why it matters: Context tells the system what to do first.

Output: A fact that is ready to route.

Digital Assets

What it is: The thing affected by the fact: customer, contract, system, document, device, or queue.

Why it matters: You can see what changed and who owns it.

Output: Updated asset data linked to the event.

Agentic Factory

What it is: The layer that runs agents, genies, and streams.

Why it matters: It moves work from start to finish in the right order.

Output: Work completed with a clear execution record.

Digital Workforce

What it is: Human workers and digital workers working together.

Why it matters: Digital workers add speed, humans add judgment.

Output: Work completed with approvals and ownership.

Digital Twin

What it is: A live view of current state and recent changes.

Why it matters: Teams can see what is happening now and what just changed.

Output: Current state plus a usable history.

New Fact

What it is: The result captured as the next fact.

Why it matters: It closes the loop and starts the next cycle.

Output: The workflow continues with updated state.

The Operating Loop

A Single, Closed, Continuous Pipeline

  1. A real event is captured as a Fact.
  2. Dimensions add context to the fact.
  3. The fact is linked to the right Digital Asset.
  4. The Agentic Factory routes and orchestrates the response.
  5. The Digital Workforce executes the required work.
  6. The Digital Twin updates current state and history.
  7. The result is captured as a New Fact, and the loop continues.
Why Real-Time Business Operating System Works for Any Business
  • Starts from facts: Teams work from what actually happened, not assumptions.
  • Uses one workflow: Every function follows the same operating loop.
  • Improves response speed: Work can move in real time, not days later.
  • Creates traceability: Decisions, actions, and outcomes are all recorded.
  • Supports step-by-step automation: You can shift work from human to digital safely.
  • Gives one operating view: Assets and events are visible across the business.
?
The universal truth: Every business runs on events (facts), applies context (dimensions), updates entities (digital assets), makes decisions (agentic factory), and executes work (hybrid workers). RTBoss simply makes this explicit, traceable, and optimizable.
