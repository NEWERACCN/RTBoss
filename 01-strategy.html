Section 1 · RTBoss Foundation

The Real-Time Business Operating System

A universal way to run any business. Not software, not a methodology, not automation — a real-time operating model that treats the entire business as a continuous stream of objective, timestamped events flowing through one consistent pipeline.

RTBoss := A real-time operating model where everything—sales, support, operations, finance, compliance, physical assets—follows one unchanging pattern: Fact → Dimensions → Digital Asset → Agentic Factory → Hybrid Worker → New Fact. This creates no-drift consistency: reality is captured uniformly, decisions are traceable, work scales without reinventing processes per department or industry.
Core Elements

Facts — Objective, Timestamped Events

Indisputable events or signals that actually happened: payment cleared at 14:32 UTC, ticket submitted, sensor anomaly detected, approval granted.

Critical principle: If it is not captured as a fact, it does not exist operationally. Verbal commitments, hallway conversations, undocumented decisions are NOT facts until written into the system.

Dimensions — Essential Context

Context added to facts to make them meaningful and actionable: who, what, where, urgency (Traffic Light: RED/YELLOW/GREEN), risk, value, jurisdiction, client tier, location.

Dimensions turn raw events into prioritized situations that can be routed and acted upon.

Digital Assets — Persistent, Versioned Entities

Everything the business owns and manages: customers, contracts, devices, buildings, documents, queues, capital.

Facts always affect one or more digital assets, making change visible, governable, and measurable over time.

Agentic Factory (DOL) — Decision & Orchestration Layer

Interprets fact + dimensions, determines the correct response, applies rules/models/policy, assigns work to the right actor (Digital Worker, Hybrid Worker, or Human Worker), and enforces governance and traceability.

The "brain" of RTBoss — where automation, intelligence, and human judgment intersect.

Hybrid Workforce — Humans Enabled by Digital Workers

Digital workers handle deterministic or high-speed tasks automatically or semi-automatically. Humans set intent, define policy, resolve ambiguity, approve sensitive actions, and retain accountability.

Current state: ~95% Human Worker, 5% Digital Worker
Target state (Q3 2026): 60% Digital Worker, 30% Hybrid Worker, 10% Human Worker

The Operating Loop

A Single, Closed, Continuous Pipeline

  1. A real event becomes a Fact
  2. Dimensions add context (Traffic Light, Worker Type, Domain, Client, etc.)
  3. The fact links to the affected Digital Asset
  4. The Agentic Factory decides and assigns (Digital/Hybrid/Human Worker)
  5. The Hybrid Worker executes (digital automation or human judgment)
  6. The outcome becomes a New Fact, restarting the loop
Why RTBoss Works for Any Business
  • Avoids the classic failure of forcing process standardization across diverse teams
  • Standardizes only reality capture — the pattern every business already follows naturally
  • Enables real-time visibility — minutes/hours response times, not days/weeks
  • Built-in controls — every fact, decision, and outcome is traceable
  • Easy scaling of AI agents — start with Human Workers, incrementally shift to Digital/Hybrid
  • Whole-business view — unified fact streams and asset updates across all departments
✦
The universal truth: Every business runs on events (facts), applies context (dimensions), updates entities (digital assets), makes decisions (agentic factory), and executes work (hybrid workers). RTBoss simply makes this explicit, traceable, and optimizable.
Section 1 · Strategic Framework

Strategic Pillars

How RTBoss delivers value today and scales tomorrow. AI Studio is the engineering foundation. The four revenue pillars are practical expressions of the identical universal pattern applied to different business contexts.

Every pillar follows: Fact → Dimensions → Digital Asset → Agentic Factory → Hybrid Worker → New Fact. The pattern is identical. The application context changes.
Pillar 01
AI Studio — Platform Development & Agent Engineering

The factory behind every vertical. AI Studio is where Mini-OS domains are designed, specialist agents are engineered, and the RTBoss loop is configured, tested, and deployed for each client context. Every engagement starts here — no vertical ships without passing through AI Studio first.

Codex 5.3 scaffolds and versions all agent systems, collapsing multi-week manual builds into governed, repeatable sessions. Opus 4.6 orchestration is tuned and validated here before it runs in production. AI Studio owns the build, the model selection, the tool bindings, and the deployment pipeline.

Why it anchors everything: Pillars 2–5 are where we earn recurring revenue. AI Studio is why clients trust we can deliver them. It is our engineering credibility — the proof that RTBoss is a repeatable capability, not a bespoke project assembled from scratch each time.

Pillar 02
MIP — Managed Intelligent Provider Services

Our current revenue foundation and the fastest path to client value. MIP turns every IT event, service request, and infrastructure alert into a structured fact — context added, asset linked, resolution routed through the Agentic Factory. Routine cases are resolved autonomously; complex ones are escalated to the right human with full context already assembled.

Delivered as a monthly retainer across three tiers: Trial ($2–4k), Standard ($4–8k), and Strategic ($8–15k). Clients receive 24/7 monitoring, predictive alerting, automatic remediation, and clear reporting on downtime prevented and costs saved. Current run rate: $38.5k MRR across four active clients.

Why it leads the revenue pillars: MIP generates the steady recurring base that funds AI Studio development, produces high-volume fact streams that improve every other pillar, and creates the trusted relationships that open doors to LiveOps, Intelligent Buildings, and Intelligence engagements.

Pillar 03
LiveOps — Real-Time Command Center & Back-of-House Operations

The operational backbone that most clients don't see until they need it. LiveOps runs the real-time command center — live view of incoming facts, active situations, asset status, and workflow health — while simultaneously managing the back-of-house human layer: call centers, support desks, security operations, incident response, and administrative teams handling judgment, escalation, and compliance.

Built on the iceberg model: the visible front-of-house outcomes clients celebrate sit above the waterline; the larger, foundational mass keeping it stable is LiveOps. Includes a Digital Twin environment for replaying history, testing changes, running simulations, and predicting outcomes before committing to production.

Why it compounds value: LiveOps is where RTBoss becomes irreplaceable. Once a client's operations run through the command center, the switching cost is near-zero to add new Mini-OS domains — every new fact type lands in an environment that already knows the client's assets, people, and patterns.

Pillar 04
Intelligent Buildings + Robotics & Physical Operations

RTBoss extended into the physical world. Sensors, devices, robots, and building management systems across offices, factories, warehouses, and facilities all generate facts — temperature spikes, equipment faults, inventory movements, energy anomalies. Intelligent Buildings applies the same loop: context added, assets linked, response decided, action executed, state updated.

Outcomes are immediate and measurable: self-managing spaces, autonomous HVAC and energy optimisation, predictive maintenance dispatch, robot coordination, and safety monitoring — all without manual intervention. Delivered as a Value Engine implementation ($60–100k) layered onto an existing MIP or LiveOps engagement.

Why it extends our reach: Physical operations unlock industries and contract sizes that pure digital RTBoss cannot address. Warehousing, logistics, manufacturing, and facility management all become addressable — each with higher-value, longer-term contracts and compelling proof points for new client acquisition.

Pillar 05
RTBoss Intelligence — Analytics, Planning & Foresight

The highest-value unlock in the RTBoss stack. Where every other pillar operates in the present — capturing facts, resolving issues, running operations — Intelligence works across time. It turns the continuous fact stream from all active pillars into real-time dashboards, anomaly detection, KPI tracking, and predictive foresight, with no ETL delays and no data silos.

Agentic intelligence agents monitor streams continuously, run scenario models against the Digital Twin, surface prescriptive recommendations, and — where governance permits — auto-adjust plans before human review. Clients get capacity planning, demand forecasting, early risk detection, and cross-department insights that were previously impossible without a dedicated analytics team.

Why it elevates every engagement: Intelligence transforms RTBoss from an operational efficiency tool into a strategic asset. Clients stop asking "what happened" and start asking "what should we do next" — and RTBoss answers with traceable, fact-backed reasoning rather than gut feel or quarterly reports.

⬡
The meta-pillar: All five pillars use the same RTBoss loop. AI Studio builds and maintains the platform. MIP handles IT facts. LiveOps orchestrates human work. Intelligent Buildings handles physical facts. RTBoss Intelligence analyzes all fact streams. The pattern never changes — only the domain of application.
